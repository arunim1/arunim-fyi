A [survey](https://www.pnas.org/doi/epub/10.1073/pnas.2419055122), which finds that "existential risk narratives about AI do not distract from its immediate harms", which roughly matches my intuition—people do not really start or stop caring about immediate harms as a result of headlines like. But should be taken with a few pinches of salt because who knows whether "exposing people to headlines" is a good proxy for the actual dynamics of the world.
